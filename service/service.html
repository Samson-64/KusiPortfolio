I am a technology and innovation strategist working with companies, governments, and development partners to design and scale digital learning and entrepreneurship systems across Africa.

Ekima has partnered with national institutions including the Tanzania Institute of Education (TIE) to build and launch the country’s first interactive digital learning platform, now serving teachers and students nationwide.

I also founded Founders Tanzania, a registered NGO and national founder community supporting underserved and grassroots entrepreneurs. The community connects founders, talent, and ecosystem enablers, has facilitated over 3,000 opportunities, and delivers practical founder training and ecosystem support.
